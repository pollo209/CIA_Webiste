Program Overview

Experiential Learning

More than just a robust classroom education, your experience at LSBE will include training and opportunities to build upon what you learn in class and apply it in real-life context. This includes internships, on-campus company interaction, Passport Program for learning soft skills, clubs, projects with local businesses, faculty research opportunities, living-learning community, competitions, and special speakers.

Program Competitive Advantage

  • Selective application process and cohort approach: Each cohort completes all four specialized courses together, participates in off-site analytics competitions, and delivers analysis results to industry partners.
  • 24/7 access to an exclusive computer lab: Specialized software and industry-standard data sets. Students utilize Excel, PowerBI, Tableau, SQL, SAS, R, and Python.
  • Partnership with corporations: Current, real-world industry projects. Sponsors have included: Hormel, Target, Maurices, and Schwan’s food company.
  • Membership in the UMD CIA Club: Invites guest speakers and participates in competitions like MUDAC.
  • Dedicated CIA Program Director: Works with each student from application through graduation.
  • Committed CIA Advisory Board: Meets several times a year to interact with students for networking and mentoring.
  • Internships: Assistance in searching, preparing, and networking. Past students have interned at Maurices, Target, Best Buy, Supervalu, Kohl's, 3M, Schwan's Food Company, Toro, Hormel Foods, US Bank, AMSOIL, Christopher and Banks, BASF, Polaris, Anderson Window, and Jack Link's.
